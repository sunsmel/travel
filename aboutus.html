Who we are?

Welcome to our travel blog! We are a couple from the USA and India, living miles apart but connected by our love for each other and love for adventure and exploring new places.

We always talk about travel and going to new places before our one trip conclude we already making plans for our next adventure. Our passion for travel brought us together and we decided to combine our unique perspectives to create a platform that inspires others to venture out into the world.

We believe that travel is not just about visiting new destinations, but about making memories and experiencing different cultures. Our travels have taken us to some of the most beautiful and exotic destinations, and we have created memories that will last a lifetime. Whether it's trying new foods, learning about different customs, or simply taking in breathtaking views, we have found that travel has a way of enriching our lives and our relationships.

Our goal is to provide you with an insight into the world through our eyes and to inspire you to step outside of your comfort zone and discover new cultures, cuisines, and experiences. Whether you're planning a romantic getaway or a family vacation, we believe that travel has the power to bring couples closer and to bring people together.

We understand the challenges of a long-distance relationship, but through our shared love of travel, we have found a way to stay connected and strengthen our bond. We hope that our blog will serve as a source of inspiration for couples everywhere, and show that no matter the distance, love and adventure can always bring you closer.

Thank you for joining us on this journey and we hope that our adventures will inspire you to make your own memories and explore the world with your loved one.

With love and wanderlust,
