Explore Saudi Arabia for Free: Visa-Free Stopover with Saudia Airways!

Saudia, the national airline of Saudi Arabia, now offers a stopover program for travellers to explore the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia while en route to their destination. The program provides a unique opportunity to discover the rich culture and history of the country, as well as to perform pilgrimage. Here is all you need to know with confirmed official terms.

If you are looking for an exciting travel adventure without spending too much money on visas and accommodations, look no further than Saudi Arabia. Saudia Airways offers a stopover program that allows travelers to book a flight with a layover in Jeddah or Riyadh for up to 96 hours, during which they can explore the beautiful country or perform Umrah. The best part? You can enjoy a free one-night stay on each side of your travel!

To take advantage of Saudia Airways' stopover program, follow these simple steps. Firstly, select your international departure and arrival destinations. Next, choose the most suitable flights with a connection time of up to 96 hours. If you do not have a valid document to enter Saudi Arabia, apply for a stopover visa from the additional services page on the Saudia website. It is important to prepare your personal photos according to the required specifications before filling out the form.

Once you have completed the booking process, you will receive an email with the issued visa and insurance for the duration of your stay in Saudi Arabia. It states that the electronic stopover visa is issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and is valid for 96 hours. However, it is important to note that obtaining the electronic stopover visa does not necessarily guarantee entry to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, as the documents will be checked by the passport officer to verify the validity of the information that was submitted.

To enjoy the free accommodation, offer, apply for a one-day free stay by clicking on the Saudia Holidays banner on the reservation confirmation page, or from manage my booking Saudia Holiday banner, or the Saudia Holidays website. When you arrive at the hotel, present your boarding pass to the hotel receptionist to benefit from the free accommodation offer.

It is important to note that a stopover visa is not accepted if the guest has a valid visa or documents allowing him to enter the Kingdom. Companions under the age of eighteen are included in the visa applicant's application, as companions of this age group are not allowed to apply individually. If the application is rejected for one of the applicants, all persons attached to the same application will be rejected accordingly. The responsibility to ensure the validity of the travel documents, the validity of the visa, the duration of the layover, health requirements, attendance for the flight before the take-off time, and the amendment of the reservation after the issuance of the visa and tickets is the responsibility of the traveler. To make it more convenient for pilgrims there is free shuttle bus service between Jeddah airport and Makkah. Airport contact is +966 920011233 for details.

Saudi Arabia has many exciting places to explore, including historical and tourist sites. The stopover program allows you to perform Umrah, visit holy sites, and enjoy other wonderful activities during your stay in Saudi Arabia. The country is known for its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and hospitality, making it an excellent destination for travelers looking for a unique and memorable travel experience. With a free one-night stay on each side of your travel, you can enjoy a memorable trip to this beautiful country. Just be sure to follow the instructions carefully and take responsibility for your travel documents and requirements. We hope you have a wonderful trip!
