Maafushi: The hub of excursions and ease

Maafushi is an escape to where turquoise waters meet powdery sand and tropical romance fills the air. Fall in love with the island's serene atmosphere and create unforgettable memories as you bask in its natural beauty.

Recently, we had the opportunity to visit Maafushi in the Maldives and it was a trip filled with adventure and unforgettable experiences. From stunning sea views to delicious local cuisine, Mafushi truly has it all. Mafushi is very popular island with a lot to offer. Half of island is administrative building however half of it well maintained filled with hotels, restaurants, excursion shops and public space with big parks and beautiful beaches.

One of the highlights of our trip was snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters around Maafushi. The vibrant coral reefs were home to a variety of exotic fish and sea creatures, and it was a thrill to swim alongside them. We also went on a fantastic speed boat ride from Male airport to Maafushi, taking in the breathtaking views of the surrounding waters and nearby resorts and islands along the way.

Another exciting excursion was visiting a shipwreck near Maafushi. The wreck was a popular spot for snorkeling, and we were able to see a variety of sea life that had made the wreck its home. It was a unique and memorable experience that we would highly recommend to anyone visiting the area.

Not to forget the greatest adventure of swimming with giant blue whales as they slide underneath us and the crazy crowd making panic situation to have a glimpse of the giant. We were lucky to spot three and swim above and near them. It is worth mentioning swimming with stingrays, manta ray and baby sharks touching and petting them, they were like our pets.

More gems of Mafushi were its sandbanks and fishing opportunities. We were able to escape the crowds and enjoy some alone time on the tranquil sandbanks, surrounded by nothing but the beauty of the ocean and the sounds of the seabirds. And, if you're a fishing enthusiast, you'll be pleased to know that Mafushi offers fantastic reef fishing opportunities. We had the opportunity to go on a fishing trip and was able to witness the stunning sunset along the beautiful coast of neighboring islands and resorts while on our way to the fishing spots.

In addition to fishing and sandbanks, we also had the opportunity to visit and have lunch on the beautiful beaches of Fulidhoo, Dhigurah, Dhangethi and more. These nearby islands were a perfect escape from the crowds, and I loved exploring their quiet, pristine beaches and taking in the breathtaking views of the surrounding waters.

When it came to food, we were impressed by the variety and quality of local and international cuisine available in Maafushi. We ate at several different restaurants during our stay and was always impressed by the fresh, flavorful dishes on offer. Whether it was grilled fish, traditional curries, or tropical fruit smoothies, all were good to try.

Of course, no visit to Mafushi would be complete without experiencing the beauty of the island's sunrises and sunsets. Whether we were relaxing on the beach, fishing, or exploring the island, we were never far from the stunning beauty of the sun as it rose and set over the water. The colors in the sky were simply breathtaking, and we found ourselves constantly stopping to take it all in.

In conclusion, Maafushi in the Maldives was a truly unforgettable experience. From the stunning sea views to the fantastic fishing, sandbanks, and snorkeling, to the delicious local cuisine and beautiful beaches, there was something for everyone. We would highly recommend a visit to anyone looking for an adventure-filled tropical paradise.
